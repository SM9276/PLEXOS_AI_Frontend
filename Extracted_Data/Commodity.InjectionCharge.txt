Header: Commodity Injection Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/~
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Cost of adding the Commodity to inventory

Paragraphs:
Commodity Injection Charge is a
          per-unit charge applied to any Injection into the inventory. The charge applies to the gross amount injected i.e. including Injection Losses .
          The cost is reported in the property Injection
            Cost .
