Header: Generator Ramp Down Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Charge applied to ramping down

Paragraphs:
Generator Ramp Down Charge is a
          charge applied to ramping down. This charge can be used to approximate
          the maintenance/wear-and-tear cost of ramping. The charge is applied
          to all Ramp between Min
            Stable Level and Max
            Capacity and also to the running down from Min
            Stable Level to zero.
          The cost incurred is reported in the property Ramp
            Down Cost .
