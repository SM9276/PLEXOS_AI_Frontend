Header: Water Zone Max Capacity Reserve Margin

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum capacity reserve margin for capacity planning

Paragraphs:
Water Zone Max Capacity Reserve Margin sets an upper bound on the capacity reserve margin in the Water Zone in LT Plan . The expansion solution will attempt to limit the capacity reserve margin below this value by restricting new builds and/or retiring water plants.
