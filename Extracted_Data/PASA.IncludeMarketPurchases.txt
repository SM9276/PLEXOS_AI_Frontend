Header: PASA Include Market Purchases

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	Include market purchases as generation capacity in PASA

Paragraphs:
PASA Include Market Purchases toggles on/off counting of Market Max Purchases as generation supply in PASA. You may want to include this element when modelling a system connected to an external market with significant import capability.
