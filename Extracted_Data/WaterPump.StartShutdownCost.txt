Description:	Cost of pump starts and shutdowns

Paragraphs:
Water Pump Start & Shutdown Cost is the cost of pump starts and shutdowns. For chronological simulation phases (see LT Plan Chronology, MT
            Schedule Chronology and ST Schedule) this is: Start & Shutdown Cost = Start Cost × Units Started
            + Shutdown Cost × Units Shut Down

For chronological simulation phases (see LT Plan Chronology, MT
            Schedule Chronology and ST Schedule) this is:

Start & Shutdown Cost = Start Cost × Units Started
            + Shutdown Cost × Units Shut Down
