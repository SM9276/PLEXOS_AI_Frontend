Header: Water Demand Water Nodes Demand Participation Factor

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Proportion of water demand that occurs at the water node

Paragraphs:
Water Demand Water Nodes Demand Participation Factor is the proportion of water Demand that occurs at the Water Node .

If the Water Demand is connected to one Water Node the default value for the participation factor is one, otherwise the default value is zero i.e. if you connect a Water Demand to multiple Water Nodes you should set these participation factors to non-zero values.
