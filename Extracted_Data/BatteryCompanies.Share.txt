Header: Battery Companies Share

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	100
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Percentage share of ownership

Paragraphs:
The ownership of a Battery can be shared between two or more Companies . The share of ownership is determined by this property.

Note:
Lists:
	- The property values must be entered as percentages like 50% not decimal values like 0.5.
	- The shares need not sum to unity.
