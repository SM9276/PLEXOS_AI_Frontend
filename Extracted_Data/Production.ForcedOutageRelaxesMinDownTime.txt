Header: Production Forced Outage Relaxes Min Down Time

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	If ramp constraints should all be formulated upfront rather than checked iteratively.

Paragraphs:
Production Forced Outage Relaxes Min Down Time controls whether or not Generator Min Down Time is enforced immediately after a unit goes out of service due to a Forced Outage .

By default the unit must continue to stay down for at least the Min Down Time hours. If you turn on this option then a unit can come back into service earlier, once the required repair time is completed.
