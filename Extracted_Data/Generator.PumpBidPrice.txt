Header: Generator Pump Bid Price

Table Details:
Units:	$/MWh
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	-10000
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Bid price of pump load in band

Paragraphs:
As an input Generator Pump Bid Price is the price part of a multi-band bid for Pump Load . The quantity is set by Pump Bid Quantity . See the Balancing Markets article for details.

As an output Generator Pump Bid Price repeats the input value in incremental style .
