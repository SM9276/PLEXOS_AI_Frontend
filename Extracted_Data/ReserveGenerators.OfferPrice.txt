Header: Reserve Generators Offer Price

Table Details:
Units:	$/MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	10000
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Reserve offer price in offer band

Paragraphs:
Reserve Reserve Generators Offer Price defines the price (premium) for provision of reserves by the generator. The quantity part of the offer is defined with Offer Quantity .

Note that by default the provision of reserve by the generator is 'priced' at opportunity cost, thus this offer price acts as a premium to that opportunity cost.
