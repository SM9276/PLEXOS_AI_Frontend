Header: Message 73

Table Details:
Severity:	Moderate (value = 3)
Default Action:	Error (value = 0)
Description:	<value> <name> initial conditions must include [<value>].

Paragraphs:
An example of where Message 73 can occur is as follows:

To define initial conditions you must include Initial Generation even if Initial Units Generating is zero.
Lists:
	- Where you define one of the Generator initial condition properties: Initial Units Generating , Initial Hours Down , Initial Hours Up , but
	- you do not define Initial Generation .
