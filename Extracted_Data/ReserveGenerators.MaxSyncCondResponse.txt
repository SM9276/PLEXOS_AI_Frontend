Description:	Maximum synchronous condenser reserve response

Paragraphs:
Reserve Generators Max Sync Cond Response defines a limit on the maximum response of each
          generating unit running in synchronous-condenser mode (also referred
          to as 'tail-water depressed' mode for hydro units). Generators capable
          of synchronous condenser mode are identified with the Sync
            Cond Units property.
