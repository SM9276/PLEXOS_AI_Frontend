Description:	First hour of each step the Condition is allowed to be active

Paragraphs:
Variable Step Hour Active From is the index of the first hour of each step the Variable is allowed to be active. This overrides any other activity-related property such as setting Profile . The range of hours over which the activity is allowed in each step is from Step Hour Active From until Step Hour Active From plus Step Hours Active minus one.
