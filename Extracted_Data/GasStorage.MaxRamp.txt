Header: Gas Storage Max Ramp

Table Details:
Units:	1000·~ (Metric), ~ (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	≥0
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum rate of change in storage per interval

Paragraphs:
Gas Storage Max Ramp is the maximum allowed rate of change (Ramp) in storage End Volume between one dispatch interval and the next. The volume is allowed to change up or down by this amount at most. By default the constraint is a hard constraint meaning that no violations are allowed.

The Ramp can also be constrained over longer time periods using the Max Ramp Hour , Max Ramp Day , Max Ramp Week , Max Ramp Month , Max Ramp Year properties.
