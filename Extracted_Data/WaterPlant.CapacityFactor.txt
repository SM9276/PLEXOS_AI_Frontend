Description:	Proportion of installed or rated capacity producing

Paragraphs:
Water Plant Capacity Factor is a
          measure of the utilization of the Water Plant. Capacity factor is defined
          as:

Capacity Factor = Production / ( Max Capacity x Units x time )

The term 'time' is the number of hours over which the
          production is measured.
