Header: Report Output Results by Fiscal Year

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	True (-1)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	If summary results are written by year

Paragraphs:
Report Output Results by Fiscal Year switches on/off writing of summary results for each fiscal year of the simulation horizon where the fiscal year is defined by the setting Year Ending .
