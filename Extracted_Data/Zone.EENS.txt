Header: Zone EENS

Table Details:
Units:	MWh
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Expected energy not served (summary type "Sum")

Paragraphs:
Zone EENS is only computed by PASA when the Compute Reliability Indices setting is 'on'.

This is calculated directly from the EDNS .  It is simply the total amount of time multiplied by the expected demand not served .
