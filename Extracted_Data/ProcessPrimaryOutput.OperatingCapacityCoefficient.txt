Paragraphs:
Process Primary
            Output Operating Capacity Coefficient applies to each Facility implementing the process through the Facility Primary Process membership.
          It is the coefficient in the production definition equation of the
          capacity of Units Operating at the Facility . This input can be
          used to define an output Commodity that is a function of the spare capacity of the Facility .
