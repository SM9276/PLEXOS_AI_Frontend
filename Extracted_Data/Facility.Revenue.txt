Paragraphs:
Facility Revenue is the revenue
          received from production of the Primary
            Output Commodity of the Primary
            Process performed by the facility. It is defined as:

Revenue = Production Ã— price

where: price is defined as
          follows: If the Facility is connected to a Flow
            Node then price is the Flow Node Price ( i.e. the locational marginal price); otherwise price is set by the Market Price for the Commodity produced.
