Header: Market Price Cap

Table Details:
Units:	$/~
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Cap on Market Price when using fixed Demand/Supply

Paragraphs:
Market Price Cap sets an upper limit
          on the settlement Price when using fixed Demand / Supply . The number of hours the cap is
          reached is reported in the property Price
            Cap Hours .
