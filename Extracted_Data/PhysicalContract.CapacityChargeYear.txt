Header: Physical Contract Capacity Charge Year

Table Details:
Units:	$/kW/yr
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Annual fixed charge for contract capacity

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Capacity Charge Year defines the physical contract purchase decision in LT Plan to be by year, meaning that the contract quantity decision can change every year of the planning horizon. The value then sets the capacity charge for purchases of generation or load capacity.
