Description:	Non-physical inflow required to maintain feasible storage volumes.

Paragraphs:
Storage Non-physical Inflow is the non-physical inflow required to maintain feasible storage volumes. Non-physical inflow is only allowed when Storage Non-physical Inflow Penalty is defined.
