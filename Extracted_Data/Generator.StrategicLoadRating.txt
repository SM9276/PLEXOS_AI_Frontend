Header: Generator Strategic Load Rating

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Pumping unit/Anti-generation rating for application in RSI capacity calculations.

Paragraphs:
Generator Strategic Load Rating is the rating for a pumping unit or Anti-generator that to be used in calculating the Region Strategic Load in addition to Region Load during the RSI bid-cost markup algorithm.

See also RSI Demand , Purchaser Strategic Load Rating and Battery Strategic Load Rating .
