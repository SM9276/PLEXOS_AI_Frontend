Header: Line Min Capacity Reserves

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	-1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Minimum amount of capacity reserves supplied to the receiving Region/Zone.

Paragraphs:
Line Min Capacity Reserves is the minimum amount of capacity reserves supplied to the receiving Region or capacity Zone where a negative indicates capacity supplied to the sending end. This limit applies to capacity flows in LT Plan and PASA .

If not defined the Line Min Rating is assumed.
