Table Details:

Default Value:	0

Key Property:	No
Description:	Coefficient of number of units retired in the year

Paragraphs:
Flow Node Constraints Units Retired in Year Coefficient is the left-hand side coefficient of the Units Retired in Year in the constraint.

With the coefficient represented by a (i,j) the following
          term is added to the formulation:

... + a (i,j) × Flow
            Node Units Retired in Year ... {≤, ≥, =} b j
