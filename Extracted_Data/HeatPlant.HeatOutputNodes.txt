Header: Heat Plant Heat Output Nodes

Table Details:
Required Members:	0
Maximum Members:	unbounded
Description:	set of heat nodes the heat plant receives from

Paragraphs:
Heat plant Heat Output Nodes is the collection of Heat Nodes that the heat plant connects and supplies heat to. A heat plant can connect to many heat nodes. Participation factor can be used to split the proportion of heat production to different heat nodes.
