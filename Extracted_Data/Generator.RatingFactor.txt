Generator Rating Factor sets the unit Rating as a percentage of Max Capacity (or Rating if Rating is defined).

Rating Factor can be defined as a multi-band property when used in conjunction with Generator Head
           Storage Efficiency Point to define a different rating depending on
           the amount of head available at a hydro generator.
