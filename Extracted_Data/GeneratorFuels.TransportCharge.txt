Header: Generator Fuels Transport Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/GJ (Metric), $/MMBtu (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Transport charge (added to base fuel price)

Paragraphs:
Generator Fuels Transport Charge covers any additional cost incurred to transport the fuel to the generator and is over and above the cost captured by Fuel Total Price . Note that this charge is first divided by the Fuel Heat Value before being applied.

This cost is also applied where the fuel is in the Start Fuels for the Generator unless over-ridden by Generator Start Fuels Transport Charge .
