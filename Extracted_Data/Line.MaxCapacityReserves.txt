Header: Line Max Capacity Reserves

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum amount of capacity reserves supplied to the receiving Region/Zone.

Paragraphs:
Line Max Capacity Reserves is the maximum amount of capacity reserves supplied to the receiving Region or capacity Zone . This limit applies to capacity flows in LT Plan and PASA .

If not defined the Line Max Rating is assumed.
