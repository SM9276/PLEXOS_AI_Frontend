Charging Station Discharge
          Price is the price received for generation from the charging station
          provided by Discharging the batteries of connected Vehicles .
          It is calculated as:

Discharge Price = 0.1 x ( Node Price . - UoS
            Charge )

Note the factor 0.1 converts the nodal price and UoS
            Charge in $/MWh to c/kWh for reporting.
