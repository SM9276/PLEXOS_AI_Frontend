Header: Vehicle VO&M Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/km (Metric), $/m (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Variable operation and maintenance charge

Paragraphs:
Vehicle VO&M Charge is the
          variable operation and maintenance charge associated with operating a
          vehicle. The charge is applied to each unit of Distance travelled (km or mile). The total cost incurred is reported in the VO&M Cost property.
