Description:	Maximum ramp down rate

Paragraphs:
Line Max Ramp Down is the limit on the amount by which the Flow may decrease each minute. If defined, this constraint is 'hard' meaning that violations are not allowed.

Currently Line Max Ramp Down can only be applied on DC lines.
