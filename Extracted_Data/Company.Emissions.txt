Header: Company Emissions

Table Details:
Required Members:	0
Maximum Members:	unbounded
Description:	set of emissions produced by the company

Paragraphs:
The Company Emissions collection is used to associate a company with a set of emissions that it has Grandfather Rights to produce.

Those rights are set by the following:
Lists:
	- Allocation HOUR
	- Allocation DAY
	- Allocation WEEK
	- Allocation MONTH
	- Allocation YEAR
