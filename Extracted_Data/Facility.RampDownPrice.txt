Description:	Shadow price of the maximum ramping down constraint

Paragraphs:
Facility Ramp Down Price is the
          shadow price of the maximum ramping down constraint defined by Max
            Ramp Down . It represents the price that the system would be
          prepared to pay to gain one more unit of ramping
            down response.
