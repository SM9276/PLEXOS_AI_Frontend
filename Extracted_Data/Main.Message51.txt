Header: Message 51

Table Details:
Severity:	Moderate (value = 3)
Default Action:	Warn (value = 1)
Description:	Generator <name> Pump Units = <value>, cannot be greater than Generator Units = <value>.

Paragraphs:
Message 51 occurs when you define the number of Generator Pump Units greater than Units . The Units input is the total number of units in the station and Pump Units should be a subset of those units.
