Header: Heat Plant VO&M Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/GJ (Metric), $/MMBtu (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Variable operation and maintenance charge

Paragraphs:
Heat Plant VO&M Charge is a component of the incremental cost of gas processing, and is used to recover maintenance costs that are a direct function of processing e.g. wear and tear and other regular equipment replacement and servicing costs.
