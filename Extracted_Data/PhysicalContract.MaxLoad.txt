Header: Physical Contract Max Load

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum load cleared on physical contract

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Max Load sets a limit on the load dispatched on the physical contract in any interval. This overrides the maximum implied by the Bid Quantity / Bid Price . You must define this property for PLEXOS to include the load from this contract in your model i.e. it is the key property used to switch the contract in/out of your simulation.
