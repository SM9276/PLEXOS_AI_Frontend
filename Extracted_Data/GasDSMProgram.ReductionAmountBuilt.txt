Key Property:	No
Description:	Demand reduction amount chosen to be implemented

Paragraphs:
Gas DSM Program Reduction Amount Built is the amount of demand reduction chosen to be implemented in the period with the highest expansion max demand reduction.
            The chosen demand reductions in other periods will scale accordingly so that same implementation level is maintained for all the periods.
