Paragraphs:
Fuel Tax property adds to the Total Price of the fuel. This property is provided so that components of a fuels price can be entered in bands. For example one component of the price might represent emissions charges.

Here there are two tax components bringing the total price of the fuel to 3 + 0.75 + 0.99 = 4.74.
