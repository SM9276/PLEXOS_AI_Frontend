Description:	Proportion of maximum capacity that must be set aside for reserves
G1	Reserve Share	15	%
G2	Reserve Share		%
G3	Reserve Share	10	%

Paragraphs:
Generator Reserve Share defines a percentage of plant capacity this should be set aside for reserve purposes. This property is not related to the detailed reserve cooptimization , but is a simpler mechanism for hardwiring a particular reserve sharing scheme.
