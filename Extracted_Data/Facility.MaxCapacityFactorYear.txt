Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum capacity factor in year

Paragraphs:
Facility Max Capacity Factor Year
          sets an upper bound on the facility's Capacity
            Factor in each year of the simulation. Years are defined by the
          starting month of the Horizon . See Max Capacity Factor for
          more details.
