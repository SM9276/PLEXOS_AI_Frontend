Header: Region MLF Adjusts Offer Price

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Mode:	Input Only

Description:	If Generator [Marginal Loss Factor] adjusts [Offer Price].

Paragraphs:
Region MLF Adjusts Offer Price switches on/off adjustment of Generator Offer Price by the Marginal Loss Factor . The same applies to Line Marginal Loss Factor , Offer Price and Line Marginal Loss Factor Back , Offer Price Back .
