Paragraphs:
Node Native Load is defined as:

Native Load = Load - Pump Load - Unserved
            Energy - Demand Curtailed - Generator Auxiliary Use - Purchaser Load - Charging
            Station Load - Negative Generation - Battery Load - Heat Plant Load (Usage) - Gas Plant Energy Consumption - Gas Storage Energy Consumption - Water Plant Energy Consumption

where: "Negative Generation" is the Generation from generators with a negative Generation
              Participation Factor
