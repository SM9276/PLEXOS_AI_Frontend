Paragraphs:
Water Plant Max Units Retired is Maximum number of units automatically retired in aggregate over the planning horizon. The cost of retiring a unit is given by Retirement Cost . Obviously the unit(s) must exist at some time in the planning horizon for there to be retirements. Thus you must define either Units or Max Units Built .
