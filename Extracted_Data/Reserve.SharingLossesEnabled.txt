Header: Reserve Sharing Losses Enabled

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Key Property:	No
Description:	If sharing of reserve accounts for transmission losses.

Paragraphs:
Reserve Sharing Losses Enabled toggles on/off modelling of transmission losses in reserve sharing . Note that losses are only accounted for when the Transmission Losses Enabled flag is also set.
