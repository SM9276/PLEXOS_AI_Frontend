Header: Gas Transport Maintenance Rate

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Expected proportion of time the Gas Transport is unavailable due to maintenance

Paragraphs:
Gas Transport Maintenance Rate is the fraction of time
              annually that the Gas Transport is expected to be out-of-service (OOS)
              due to scheduled maintenance events.
