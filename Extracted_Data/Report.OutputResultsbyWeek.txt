Header: Report Output Results by Week

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	If summary results are written by week

Paragraphs:
Report Output Results by Week switches on/off writing of summary results for each week of the simulation horizon where the week is defined by the setting Week Beginning .
