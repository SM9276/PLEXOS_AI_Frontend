Region Constraint Payments Compatibility controls the algorithm used to compute constraint payments .

An algorithm that mimics operation of a cost-based pool (similar to the Korean CBP).

An algorithm similar to the Irish SEM.

Constraint payments apply to the uniform pricing algorithm only.
