Description:	The total electric consumption of the Gas Plant

Paragraphs:
Gas Plant Energy Consumption is the total electric consumption of the Gas Plant. Electrical energy is needed to operate and maintain Gas Plants, so this is modeled as a demand variable in the gas-electricity co-optimization models.
