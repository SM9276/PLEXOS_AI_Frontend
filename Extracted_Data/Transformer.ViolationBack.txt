Description:	Violation of the flow limit in the counter-reference direction.

Paragraphs:
[Main.Transformer|Transformer]] Violation Back is the aggregate ( GWh ) violation, in the summary period (day/week/month/year), of the Transformer Rating for flows in the counter-reference direction.

Violations of flow limits are allowed only if the LimitPenalty is defined.
