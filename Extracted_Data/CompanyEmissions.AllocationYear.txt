Header: Company Emissions Allocation Year

Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Emission rights allocation
GENCO_A	CO2	5000000	tons
GENCO_B	CO2	12500000	tons

Paragraphs:
Company Emissions Allocation Year sets the annual amount of Grandfather Rights the company has to emit the given emission. These rights are accounted for in the calculation of Emissions Cost . The resulting change in Net Profit will affect the way the company bids in LRMC (Revenue Recovery) mode.

Note that the units of this allocation are those of the Summary Emission Unit set on the Units settings for the current database.
