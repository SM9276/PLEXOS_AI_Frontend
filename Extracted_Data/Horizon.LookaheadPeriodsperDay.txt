Header: Horizon Look-ahead Periods per Day

Table Details:
Units:	-
Default Value:	24
Validation Rule:	Between 1 And 86400
Description:	Number of intervals in each trading day of the look-ahead

Paragraphs:
Horizon Look-ahead Periods per Day sets the resolution (number of intervals in each modelled day) of the ST Schedule look-ahead . This must be the same or smaller than Periods per Day .
