Header: Market Max Purchases Day

Table Details:
Units:	~
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum purchase from the market each day

Paragraphs:
Market Max Purchases Day creates a Constraint object that sets an upper
          bound on the Purchases from the
          Market each day. By default this is a 'hard' constraint.
