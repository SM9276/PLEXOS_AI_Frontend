Header: Data File Historical Year Ending

Table Details:
Units:	-
Default Value:	12
Validation Rule:	Between 1 And 12
Description:	Month that years end in the historical data

Paragraphs:
Data File Historical Year Ending is the month that years end in the historical data. By default, a "year" ends in December (the 12th month) but if the historical year begins on July 1 then each year will end on June 30, the Historical Year Ending property should be set to 6 instead.
