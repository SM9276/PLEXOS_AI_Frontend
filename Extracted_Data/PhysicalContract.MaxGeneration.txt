Header: Physical Contract Max Generation

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum generation cleared on physical contract

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Max Generation sets a limit on the generation dispatched from the physical contract in any interval. This overrides the maximum implied by the sum of Offer Quantity / Offer Price . You must define this property for PLEXOS to include the generation from this contract in your model i.e. it is the key property used to switch the contract in/out of your simulation.
