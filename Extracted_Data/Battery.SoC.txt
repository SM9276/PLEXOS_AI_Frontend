Header: Battery SoC

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	State of Charge

Paragraphs:
Battery SoC is the stored Energy in the battery divided by the commissioned Capacity (i.e. before any Capacity Degradation ).

Notice that the value of SoC might be out of the range [0%, 100%] as between balance periods the storage bounds are not enforced.
