Header: Region Distributed Maintenance

Table Details:

Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maintenance notionally allocated to period

Paragraphs:
Region Distributed Maintenance is reported by PASA . It is the Maintenance allocated by the optimization in order to level the regional Capacity Reserves .

Note that this maintenance allocation is purely notional. It is subsequently used to produce the Maintenance Factor which acts to direct maintenance scheduling into appropriate high reserve periods.
