Header: Heat Plant Fuels Mutually Exclusive

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Key Property:	No
Description:	If the Fuel cannot be mixed with other Fuels associated with this heat plant.

Paragraphs:
Heat Plant Fuels Mutually Exclusive is if the Fuel cannot be mixed with other Fuels associated with this Heat Plant.

Possible values:

False= '0' Fuel can be used in combination with other fuels (default).

True = '-1' Fuel cannot be mixed with other fuels.
