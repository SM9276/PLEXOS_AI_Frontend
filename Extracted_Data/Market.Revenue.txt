Header: Market Revenue

Table Details:
Units:	$
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Value of sales to the market

Paragraphs:
Market Revenue is the value of sales into the market. The definition of Revenue depends on the
          Market Is Marginal flag.

If Is Marginal is true then:

Revenue = Sales × Price

else:

Revenue = ∑ b Sales b × Price b

where: b indexes the band of Price
