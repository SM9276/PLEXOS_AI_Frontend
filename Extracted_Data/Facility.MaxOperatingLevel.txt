Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum unit operating capacity

Paragraphs:
Facility Max Operating Level
          defines the nominal Production capacity of each unit at the facility. Together with Units this defines the Installed
            Capacity of the facility. The Unit of Max Operating Level is controlled by the Facility Capacity Basis setting and
          is either based on the Primary
            Output or Primary Input Commodity of the Primary
            Process performed by the facility.
