Header: Zone Max Capacity Reserve Margin

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum capacity reserve margin for capacity planning

Paragraphs:
Zone Max Capacity Reserve Margin sets an upper bound on the Capacity Reserve Margin in the Zone in LT Plan . The expansion solution will attempt to limit the Capacity Reserve Margin below this value by restricting new builds and/or retiring plant. By default the constraint is 'hard' meaning that it cannot be violated, but the limit can be made 'soft' by setting  the Capacity Excess Price .
