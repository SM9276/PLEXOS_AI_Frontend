Header: Generator Heat Output Nodes Participation Factor

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1
Validation Rule:	Between -1 And 1
Key Property:	No
Description:	Proportion sent to the node

Paragraphs:
Generator Heat Output Nodes Participation Factor is the proportion of generator Heat Production sent to the Heat Node . Using this property you can split heat across multiple Heat Node objects.
