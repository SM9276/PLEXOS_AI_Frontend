Paragraphs:
Gas Contract Quantity day is the quantity of gas specified in the contract to be supplied at a particular price.
            It is maximum amount of gas that a contract can produce daily. A contract can produce less than this, but not more.

When the gas contract is an expansion candidate, the original Quantity day property may be overwritten by the results of expansion based on the Expansion Quantity Day .
