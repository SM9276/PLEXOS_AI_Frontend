Header: Company Max Maintenance Factor

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	100
Validation Rule:	Between 0 and 1
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum generation capacity allowed to be scheduled on maintenance as a proportion of installed capacity

Paragraphs:
Company Max Maintenance Factor is an alternative to Max Maintenance where the constraint is expressed as a proportion of the Generator Capacity Reserves rather than absolute megawatt.
