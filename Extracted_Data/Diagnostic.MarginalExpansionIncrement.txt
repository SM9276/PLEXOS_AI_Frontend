Header: Diagnostic Marginal Expansion Increment

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Default Value:	1000
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Description:	Increment used for load in the region marginal expansion unit diagnostic.

Paragraphs:
Diagnostic Marginal Expansion Increment is a parameter of the Marginal Expansion Unit diagnostic. It sets the amount by which the load is incremented when determining the marginal expansion Generator .
