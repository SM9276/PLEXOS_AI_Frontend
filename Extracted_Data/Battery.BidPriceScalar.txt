Header: Battery Bid Price Scalar

Table Details:

Description:	Scalar applied to the [Bid Price] property

Paragraphs:
Battery Bid Price Scalar is a scaling factor applied to the defined Bid Price property. It performs the same function as Offer Price Scalar but for bids.
