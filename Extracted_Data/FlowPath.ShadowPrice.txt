Description:	Shadow price

Paragraphs:
Flow Path Shadow Price is the dual
          variable value associated with any binding Max
            Flow or Min Flow constraint. It can be interpreted as the economic benefit of relaxing
          the given limit by one unit. In summary reporting, Shadow
            Price Forward reports the average price for Max
            Flow constraint while Shadow Price Back reports on the Min
            Flow constraint.
