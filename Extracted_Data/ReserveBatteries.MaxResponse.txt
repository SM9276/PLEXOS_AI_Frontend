Description:	Maximum reserve response from discharging generation adjustment

Paragraphs:
Reserve Batteries Max Response is the maximum reserve response through adjustments to a battery's Generation level.

The maximum reserve response from adjustments to a battery's Load level can is limited by Max Charge Response .

The total maximum reserve response from the entire battery (load + generation) can be limited by Total Response .
