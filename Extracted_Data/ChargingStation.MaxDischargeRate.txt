Header: Charging Station Max Discharge Rate

Table Details:
Units:	kW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum discharge rate

Paragraphs:
Charging Stations Max
          Discharge Rate is the maximum amount of power that can be sent to the
          grid for each unit at the charging station. The total discharge rate
          for the charging station is Units x Max Discharge Rate.
