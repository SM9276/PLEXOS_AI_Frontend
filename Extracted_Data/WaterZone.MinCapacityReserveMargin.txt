Header: Water Zone Min Capacity Reserve Margin

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	-1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Minimum capacity reserve margin for capacity planning

Paragraphs:
Water Zone Min Capacity Reserve Margin sets a lower bound on the capacity reserve margin in the Water Zone in LT Plan . The expansion solution will attempt to meet the required capacity reserve margin by new builds and/or restricting water plant retirements.
