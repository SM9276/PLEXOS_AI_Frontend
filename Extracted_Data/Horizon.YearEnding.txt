Header: Horizon Year Ending

Table Details:
Units:	-
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 12
Description:	Last month of the fiscal year

Paragraphs:
This property defines years for the purpose of summary reporting and for constraint period type of year. By default a "year" runs from the beginning month as set by Horizon Date From for 12 months e.g. if the planning horizon begins on April 1 then each year will end on March 31, but if the Year Ending property is set to 12 each year will end on December 31.
