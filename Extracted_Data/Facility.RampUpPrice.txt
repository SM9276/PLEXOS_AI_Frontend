Description:	Shadow price of the maximum ramping up constraint

Paragraphs:
Facility Ramp Up Price is the
          shadow price of the maximum ramping up constraint defined by Max
            Ramp Up . It represents the price that the system would be
          prepared to pay to gain one more unit of ramping
            up response.
