Header: Transmission Report Enabled

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	True (-1)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	If transmission reporting is enabled.

Paragraphs:
Transmission Report Enabled is the master on/off switch for transmission reporting. Toggling this switch off will turn off reporting for nodes , lines , interfaces , transformers , and Flow Controls .
