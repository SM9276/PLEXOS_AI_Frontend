Header: Fuel Contract Quantity

Table Details:

Description:	Contract quantity

Paragraphs:
This is the maximum amount of the Fuel available in each simulation period and represents a hard limit. Each unit of the fuel used up to this quantity is priced according to the Fuel Contract Price .
