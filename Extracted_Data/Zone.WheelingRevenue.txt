Description:	Wheeling revenue on exports from the zone

Paragraphs:
Zone Wheeling Revenue is the revenue earned based on net exports to other Zones as defined by the inter-Zonal Wheeling Charge .

Zone export wheeling revenue defined by Wheeling Charge is included.

See also Zone Wheeling Cost .
