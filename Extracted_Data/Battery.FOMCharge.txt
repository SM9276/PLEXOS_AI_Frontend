Header: Battery FO&M Charge

Description:	Fixed operations and maintenance charge

Paragraphs:
Battery FO&M Charge is the fixed operations and maintenance charge associated with operating a BESS.  The behaviour of this property is equivalent to the Generator FO&M Charge .
