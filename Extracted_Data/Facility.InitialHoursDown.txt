Paragraphs:
Facility Initial Hours Down is
          used to set the initial ( t =0) operating state of a
          single-unit facility and is used in combination with Min
            Up Time , Min Down Time , Max Up Time , Max
            Down Time or multi-band Start
            Cost . If you define this property you must also define Initial
            Production . Only one of Initial Hours Down and Initial
            Hours Up can be non-zero.
