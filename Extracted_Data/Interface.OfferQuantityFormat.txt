Description:	Format for [Offer Quantity] and [Offer Price]
Detail:	

Paragraphs:
Interface Offer Quantity Format sets the format for Offer Quantity and Offer Price .

The total quantity is the sum of offer/bid quantity across the bands. You must define the same number of bands for quantity and price.

The total is the offer quantity/bid in the top band. You must define one more quantity band than price band.
