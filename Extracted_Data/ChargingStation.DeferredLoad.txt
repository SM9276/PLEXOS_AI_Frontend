Paragraphs:
Charging Station Deferred
          Load is the total charging load from connected Vehicles that is deferred into future periods. This deferred load adds to Charging in those future periods. Deferred load occurs to minimize the total
          system cost i.e. load can be deferred into a periods with a
          lower electric price.
