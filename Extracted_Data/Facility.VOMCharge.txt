Header: Facility VO&M Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Variable operation and maintenance charge

Paragraphs:
Facility VO&M Charge is a
              component of the incremental cost of production used to recover
              maintenance costs that are a direct function of production e.g. wear and tear and other regular equipment replacement
              and servicing costs. This is a component of a facility's Marginal
                Cost .

The variable operations and maintenance cost associated with the Production is reported in
              the property VO&M Cost and is part of the Production
                Cost.

As Output VO&M Charge reports the input value.
