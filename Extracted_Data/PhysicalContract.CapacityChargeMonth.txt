Header: Physical Contract Capacity Charge Month

Table Details:
Units:	$/kW/month
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Monthly fixed charge for contract capacity

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Capacity Charge Month defines the physical contract purchase decision in LT Plan to be by month, meaning that the contract quantity decision can change every month of the planning horizon. The value then sets the capacity charge for purchases of generation or load capacity.
