Description:	Marginal cost of production

Paragraphs:
Facility Marginal Cost is the cost
          of producing the last/next unit of the Primary
            Output Commodity of the Primary
            Process performed at the Facility. It is defined as:

Marginal Cost = Primary
            Process Marginal Cost + VO&M Charge
