Header: PASA Include Contract Generation

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	Include physical contract generation capacity in PASA

Paragraphs:
PASA Include Contract Generation toggles on/off inclusion of Physical Contract Generation Capacity in the calculation of Region Capacity Reserves and Compute Reliability Indices .
