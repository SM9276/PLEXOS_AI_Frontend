Header: Line Export Limit

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum flow allowed on the line

Paragraphs:
Line Export Limit is the maximum flow allowed on the line and accounts for Max Flow and Max Rating inputs: Export Limit = MIN( Max Flow , Max Rating )

Note: If Line Constraints are in place that restrict line flow, this can change the maximum allowable flow on the line and the reported export limit.
