Default Value:

Key Property:	No
Description:	Portion of available technical capacity dispatched

Paragraphs:
Generator Energy Utilisation Factor is the ratio of actual output over a period to the maximum possible output over that period, only for committed operational hours. The maximum possible output accounts for generator start ups, shut downs, and ramping constraints.
