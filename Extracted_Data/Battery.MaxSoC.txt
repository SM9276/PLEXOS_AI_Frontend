Paragraphs:
Battery Max SoC is the allowable maximum State of Charge.  This is expressed as a percentage of the commissioned Capacity (i.e. before any Capacity Degradation ).

Example:

The maximum state of charge that this BESS could obtain is 90% of 25 GWh , which is 22.5 GWh .
