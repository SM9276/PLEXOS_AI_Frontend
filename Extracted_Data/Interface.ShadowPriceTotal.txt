Paragraphs:
Interface Shadow Price Total in period-level reporting is the dual variable associated with the interface limits ( Max Flow and Min Flow ). In summary reporting it is the average shadow price from all periods where the Flow is in the reference direction-the counter-reference direction shadow price is reported as Shadow Price Back . Includes Shadow Price for base case plus sum of post-contingency shadow prices, for all contingencies that the element is binding under.
