Header: Physical Contract Capacity Charge Day

Table Details:
Units:	$/kW/day
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Daily fixed charge for contract capacity

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Capacity Charge Day defines the physical contract purchase decision in LT Plan to be by day, meaning that the contract quantity decision can change every day of the planning horizon. The value then sets the capacity charge for purchases of generation or load capacity.
