Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only

Default Value:	0

Description:	Repair time function shape parameter (for Weibull,lognormal)

Paragraphs:
Battery Repair Time Shape sets the shape parameter of the repair time distribution in the Monte Carlo outage simulation.

The distribution types are discussed further in the article Planned and Random Outages .
