Description:	Consumption of the Commodity

Paragraphs:
Vehicle Commodities Consumption is the amount of the Commodity consumed by the vehicle in
          the Unit of that Commodity. It is
          defined as:

Consumption = Energy
            Consumption x 3.6 / Commodity Energy
            Density
