Description:	Average cost for gas in entire model

Paragraphs:
Gas Zone Weighted Average Cost is the difference between variable costs and revenue per gas served, such as (Total Variable Costs - Total Revenue) / Total Gas Served.

Weighted Average Cost = ( Total Variable Costs - Total Revenue) / Total Gas Served.
