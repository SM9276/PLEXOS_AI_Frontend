Header: Battery Discharging VO&M Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/MWh
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Variable operation and maintenance charge for discharging

Paragraphs:
Battery Discharging VO&M Charge is the variable operation and maintenance charge for Discharging . In particular, it is the additional VO&M charge which is combined with the Battery VO&M Charge , while the battery is discharging. If the Battery VO&M Charge is not provided, 
                then only Discharging VO&M Charge applies to the gross amount of discharging.
