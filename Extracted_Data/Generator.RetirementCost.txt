Description:	Cost of retiring a unit

Paragraphs:
Generator Retirement Cost is
              the one-time cost of retiring a unit in LT
                Plan .

As output Retirement Cost is defined as:

Retirement Cost (output) = Retirement Cost (input) x Units
                Retired
