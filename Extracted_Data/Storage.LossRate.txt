Header: Storage Loss Rate

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Rate of loss due to evaporation, leakage, etc

Paragraphs:
Storage Loss Rate is the rate of loss due to evaporation, leakage, etc . This is expressed as a percentage loss per hour and is calculated against the average of Initial Volume and End Volume .
