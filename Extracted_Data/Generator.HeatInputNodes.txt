Header: Generator Heat Input Nodes

Table Details:
Required Members:	0
Maximum Members:	unbounded
Description:	set of heat nodes supplying heat to the generator

Paragraphs:
Generator Heat Input Nodes is the collection of Heat Nodes that the generator connects and receives heat from. Heat received from these heat nodes will help to off-set its fuel off-take.
A generator can connect to many heat nodes. The total amount of received heat will be the sum of the heat supplied from all the heat input nodes.
