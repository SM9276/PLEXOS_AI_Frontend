Description:	Number of hours line is congested in the reference direction

Paragraphs:
Line Hours Congested Total is the number of
          hours the Line Flow is at or above its Max Flow or at or below its Min
            Flow . The summary version of this property reports the number of
          hours at Max Flow and Hours
            Congested Back the number of hours at Min
            Flow . Includes hours congested for base case and post-contingent hours.
