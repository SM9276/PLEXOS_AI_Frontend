Description:	Own load + load contracts

Paragraphs:
Company Load is the total load assigned to the company from all sources. This includes region load, purchaser load, and physical contract load. The company must be assigned to a region or at least one node in the nodes collection for the company must be assigned to a pool in order for this output to be calculated.
