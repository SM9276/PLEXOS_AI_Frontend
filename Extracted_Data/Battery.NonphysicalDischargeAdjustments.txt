Description:	Non-physical discharge adjustments required to maintain feasible states of charge.

Paragraphs:
Battery Non-physical Discharge Adjustments is the non-physical discharging required to maintain feasible states of charge ( SoC ).
                Non-physical discharging is only allowed when Battery Non-physical Discharge Penalty is defined.
