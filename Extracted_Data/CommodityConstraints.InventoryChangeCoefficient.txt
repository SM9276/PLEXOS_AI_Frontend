Header: Commodity Constraints Inventory Change Coefficient

Table Details:
Units:	~
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Coefficient of change in the amount of the Commodity in inventory

Paragraphs:
Commodity Constraints Inventory Change Coefficient sets the coefficient of Inventory Change in the Constraint.

With the coefficient represented by a (i,j) the following
          term is added to the formulation:

... + a (i,j) × Commodity Inventory Change ... {≤,
            ≥, =} b j
