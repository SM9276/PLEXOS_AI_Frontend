Header: Line Available Transfer Capability

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Available transfer capability on the line.

Paragraphs:
Line Available Transfer Capability is Available transfer capability remaining on the line. Reported Available Transfer Capability will be on the same direction as the current line Flow .

Line Available Transfer Capability is calculated by line Flow f 0 and PTDFs .

If SCUC Enabled , then Line Available Transfer Capability is calculate from the post-contingency power flow f x and the contingency shifter factors CSF .
