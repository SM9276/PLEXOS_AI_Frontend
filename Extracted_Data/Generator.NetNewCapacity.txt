Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Net Capacity (cumulative Max Capacity x (Units Built - Units Retired))

Paragraphs:
Generator Net New Capacity is the
          net addition to Installed
            Capacity associated with new builds and retirements up to given
          period, and is defined as:

Net New Capacity t = ∑ k ≤ t ( Units
            Built k - Units
            Retired k ) × Max
            Capacity t
