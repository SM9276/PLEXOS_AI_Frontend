Units:	$/~

Description:	Penalty for violation of [Min Consumption] constraints

Paragraphs:
Commodity Min Consumption Penalty
          sets the penalty for violating the Min
            Consumption constraint and its period variants. The default
          value of -1 means 'hard constraint'.
