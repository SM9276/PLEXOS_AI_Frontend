Header: Battery Charging VO&M Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/MWh
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Variable operation and maintenance charge for charging

Paragraphs:
Battery Charging VO&M Charge is the variable operation and maintenance charge for Charging . In particular, it is the additional VO&M charge which is combined with the Battery VO&M Charge , while the battery is charging. If the Battery VO&M Charge is not provided, then 
                only Charging VO&M Charge applies to the gross amount of charging.
