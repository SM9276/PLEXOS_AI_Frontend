Commodity WACC is the
          weighted-average cost of capital for the project.

WACC is used in combination with Economic
            Life in LT Plan to compute the
          stream of annuities equivalent to the defined Build
            Cost . If no WACC rate is set, the discount
            rate is used.

See Facility WACC for more details.
