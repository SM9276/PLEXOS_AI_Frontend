Header: Market Price Floor

Table Details:
Units:	$/~
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	-1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Floor on Market Price when using fixed Demand/Supply

Paragraphs:
Market Price Floor sets a lower
          limit on the settlement Price when using fixed Demand / Supply . The number of hours the floor
          is reached is reported in the property Price
            Floor Hours .
