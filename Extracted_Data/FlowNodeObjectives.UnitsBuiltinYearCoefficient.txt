Description:	Coefficient of number of units built in the year

Paragraphs:
Flow Node Objectives Units Built in Year Coefficient is the left-hand side coefficient of the Units Built in Year in the constraint.

With the coefficient represented by a (i,j) the following
          term is added to the formulation:

... + a (i,j) × Flow
            Node Units Built in Year ... {≤, ≥, =} b j
