Description:	Technical lifetime of the gas pipeline

Paragraphs:
Gas Pipeline Technical Life is used in LT Plan and is the technical lifetime of the gas field.  The field is assumed to produce gas from its time of build until the end of its technical life. After that time its Units will be set to zero.
