Header: Zone Min Capacity Reserves

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	-1E+30
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Minimum capacity reserves allowed

Paragraphs:
Zone Min Capacity Reserves sets a lower bound on the Capacity Reserves in the Zone in LT Plan . The expansion solution will attempt to meet the required Capacity Reserves by new builds and/or restricting plant retirements. By default the constraint is 'hard' meaning that it cannot be violated, but the limit can be made 'soft' by setting  the Capacity Shortage Price .
