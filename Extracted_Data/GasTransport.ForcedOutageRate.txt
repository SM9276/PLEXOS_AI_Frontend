Header: Gas Transport Forced Outage Rate

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Expected proportion of time the Gas Transport is unavailable due to forced outage

Paragraphs:
Gas Transport Forced Outage Rate
              sets the expected level of unplanned outages, which result in a
              partial or complete loss of Gas Transport capacity for a certain
              period of time.
