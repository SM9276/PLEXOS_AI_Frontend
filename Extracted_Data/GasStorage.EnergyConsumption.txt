Header: Gas Storage Energy Consumption

Table Details:
Units:	MWh
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	total energy consumption of the Gas Storage

Paragraphs:
Gas Storage Energy Consumption is the total energy consumption of the Gas Storage based on the Gas Storage Power Consumption , Gas Storage Injection Energy Consumption , Gas Storage Volume Power Consumption , and Gas Storage Max Storage Power Consumption inputs. The Gas Storage must be connected to a Node for any electrical energy to be consumed.
