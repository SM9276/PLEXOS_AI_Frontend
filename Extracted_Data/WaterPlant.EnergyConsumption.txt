Description:	The total electric and heat consumption of the Water Plant

Paragraphs:
Water Plant Energy Consumption is the total electric and heat consumption of the Water Plant.  Electrical energy is needed to operate and maintain Water Plants , so this is modeled as a demand variable in the water-electricity co-optimization models.
