Header: Constraint Feasibility Repair Weight

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0.01
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Weight applied to relaxing the constraint in feasibility repair. Lower values mean less penalty to relax the constraint. -1 means the constraint cannot be relaxed.

Paragraphs:
Constraint Feasibility Repair Weight is the weight (penalty) applied to relaxing the Constraint in Feasibility Repair . Lower values mean less penalty to relax the Constraint . -1 means the constraint cannot be relaxed.
