Description:	Step type for look-ahead in chronological model

Paragraphs:
Horizon Look-ahead Type in combination with Look-ahead At a Time controls the length of the ST Schedule Look-ahead Indicator . It can take the following values:

The look-ahead At a Time is in units of intervals as defined by Look-ahead Periods per Day .

The look-ahead At a Time is in units of days.

The look-ahead At a Time is in units of weeks.
