Description:	Number of iterations used to recover fixed costs.

Paragraphs:
Competition Revenue Targeting Iterations controls the number of iterations (bidding rounds) modelled in attempting to meet revenue targets set by either the LRMC Recovery or Nash-Cournot games.

Increasing the iterations is like assuming more collusion between firms. Hence more than two or three iterations is probably unrealistic.
