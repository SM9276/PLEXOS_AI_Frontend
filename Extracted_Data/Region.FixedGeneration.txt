Region Fixed Generation represents embedded or 'behind-the-meter' generation/load. See Node Fixed Generation for details. Fixed generation is assigned to the nodes in the region according to their load participation factors .

In this example a fixed amount of embedded generation will be counted in the region post 1/01/2008.
