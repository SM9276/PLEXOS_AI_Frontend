Header: Vehicle Constraints Distance Coefficient

Table Details:
Units:	km (Metric), m (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Coefficient of distance travelled by the vehicle

Paragraphs:
Vehicle Constraints Distance Coefficient is the left-hand side coefficient of Distance travelled by the vehicle in the Constraint.

With the coefficient represented by a (i,j) the following
          term is added to the formulation:

... + a (i,j) × Vehicle Distance ... {≤,
            ≥, =} b j
