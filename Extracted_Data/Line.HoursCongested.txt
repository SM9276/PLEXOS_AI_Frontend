Description:	Number of hours line is congested in the reference direction in pre-contingency state

Paragraphs:
Line Hours Congested is the number of
          hours the Line Flow is at or above its Max Flow or at or below its Min
            Flow . The summary version of this property reports the number of
          hours at Max Flow and Hours
            Congested Back the number of hours at Min
            Flow . Only includes hours congested for base case, i.e in pre-contingency state.
