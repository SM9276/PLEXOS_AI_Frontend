Description:	Maximum number of units allowed to be retired in aggregate over the planning horizon

Paragraphs:
Battery Max Units Retired is the maximum number of units automatically retired in aggregate over the planning horizon by LT Plan . The cost of retiring a unit is given by Retirement Cost . Obviously the unit(s) must exist at some time in the planning horizon for there to be retirements. Thus you must define either Units or Max Units Built .

See also: Battery Max Units Retired in Year , Min Units Retired .
