Header: Gas Demand Shortage

Table Details:
Units:	GJ (Metric), MMBtu (U.S. Imperial)

Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Shortage of gas supply

Paragraphs:
Gas Demand Shortage is the amount by which supply of gas falls short of the Demand . This shortage has a notional price set by the Shortage Price . The associated cost is reported in the Shortage Cost property.
