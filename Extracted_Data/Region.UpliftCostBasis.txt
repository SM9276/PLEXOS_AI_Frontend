Header: Region Uplift Cost Basis

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1
Validation Rule:	In (1,2)
Key Property:	No
Description:	Basis for calculating generation cost for uplift calculations (cost-based or bid-base)

Paragraphs:
Region Uplift Cost Basis controls how generation cost is calculated when uplift is enabled .

In general one would set this to Bid-based when emulating the dispatch and pricing of a market using historical generator offers, but for forecasting it is more appropriate to use Cost-based.

See also the article Uplift Methods .
