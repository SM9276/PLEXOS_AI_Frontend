Header: Generator Pump Efficiency

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	70
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Efficiency of pumping

Paragraphs:
Generator Pump Efficiency is the round-trip efficiency of the pump storage generator expressed as a percentage value. Typical values lie in the range 60 - 80%. Along with Pump Load and connection to a Head Storage this property designates a Generator as a pumped storage generator.
