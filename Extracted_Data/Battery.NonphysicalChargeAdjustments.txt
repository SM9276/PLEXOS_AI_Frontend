Description:	Non-physical charge adjustments required to maintain feasible states of charge.

Paragraphs:
Battery Non-physical Charge Adjustments is the non-physical charging required to maintain feasible states of charge ( SoC ).
                Non-physical charging is only allowed when Battery Non-physical Charge Penalty is defined.
