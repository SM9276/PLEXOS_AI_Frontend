Description:	Maximum bid quantity when auto-generating steps

Paragraphs:
Gas Demand Max Bid Quantity is the maximum bid quantity when auto-generating steps. 
          If this property isn't inputted, the default value is the x-intercept of the demand step elasticity line provided by the Bid Slope , Bid Quantity , and Bid Price ,
