Description:	Generic annual fixed charge

Paragraphs:
Line Fixed Charge is a generic annual fixed charge that is treated similarly to FO&M Charge in LT Plan . The multiple bands provide a convenient way to separate various fixed costs such as refurbishment costs from other fixed operational costs.
