Description:	Provision of regulation raise reserve

Paragraphs:
Vehicle Regulation Raise Reserve is
          the provision of regulation raise reserve. This is provided in the
          same manner as Raise Reserve but to regulation type services. Note that provision of raise and
          regulation raise services is mutually exclusive by default i.e. the capacity reserved for one service cannot also be applied to the
          other.
