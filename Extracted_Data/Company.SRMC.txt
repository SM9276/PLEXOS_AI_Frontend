Paragraphs:
Company SRMC is the weighted average SRMC of the company sent out generation.

For example, if a company has 2 generators with the following outputs:

Then:

Company [SRMC] =  ( 5 x 100 x 100% + 10 x 300 x 100% ) / ( 100 x 100% + 300 x 100% ) =  8.75 ($/ MWh ).
