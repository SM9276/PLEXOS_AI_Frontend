Header: Market Max Purchases Year

Table Details:
Units:	~

Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum purchase from the market each year

Paragraphs:
Market Max Purchases Year creates a Constraint object that sets an
          upper bound on the Purchases from
          the Market each year. By default this is a 'hard' constraint.
