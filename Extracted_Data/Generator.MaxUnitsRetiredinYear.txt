Generator Max Units Retired in
          Year set the maximum number of units automatically retired in any
          single year of the planning horizon in LT
            Plan . The value should be less than or equal to the total units
          allowed to retire which is set by Max
            Units Retired .

In this example the sole unit must retire at some time in the planning
          horizon, but cannot retire between the years 2010-15.
