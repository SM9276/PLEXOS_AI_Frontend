Header: Battery Forced Outage Rate

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Expected proportion of time the facility is unavailable due to forced outage

Paragraphs:
Battery Forced Outage Rate sets the expected level of unplanned outages, which result in a loss of charge/discharge capability for a certain period of time.

See the article Planned and Random Outages for details.

As output, Forced Outage Rate is
              the percentage of time the unit is out-of-service in any given
              period.
