Header: Generator Boiler Heat Rate Incr

Table Details:
Units:	GJ/GJ (Metric), MMBtu/MMBtu (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Incremental heat rate for heat production from boiler

Paragraphs:
Generator Boiler Heat Rate Incr is the incremental heat rate for heat production from the boiler of a CHP generator . It represents the constant efficiency rate of heat production of the Auxiliary Boiler in CHP power plants.

This value is taken into account when computing the total Generators Fuel Offtake or Fuel Offtake .
