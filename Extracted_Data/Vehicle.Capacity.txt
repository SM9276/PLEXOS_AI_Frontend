Paragraphs:
Vehicle Capacity is the battery
            size for an electric or hybrid-electric vehicle in kilowatt-hours.
            To model an internal combustion engined (ICE) car, simply omit this
            property.

As output Vehicle Capacity is the
            total energy storage of the vehicle and is defined as:

Capacity (output) = Capacity (input) x Units
