Header: Company Min Maintenance Factor

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Between 0 and 1
Key Property:	No
Description:	Generation capacity that must be scheduled on maintenance as a proportion of installed capacity

Paragraphs:
Company Max Maintenance is an alternative to Min Maintenance where the constraint is expressed as a proportion of the Generator Capacity Reserves rather than absolute megawatt.
