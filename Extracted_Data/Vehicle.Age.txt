Paragraphs:
Vehicle age is the number of cycles completed by each Vehicle's battery. An average across all units in the Vehicle object is given.

More detail cycles including a formula and examples are given in Battery Age .

A detailed Vehicle model is necessary for Vehicle energy storage limits to be modelled. See Vehicle for a comparison 
                of simple and detailed vehicle models (including necessary properties that must be defined etc.).
