Header: Region Price of Dump Energy

Table Details:
Units:	$/MWh
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	-1000
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Price of dump energy per MWh (1=hard constraint)

Paragraphs:
Region Price of Dump Energy is the notional penalty price for Dump Energy , which occurs when Generation exceeds the Load . This should be input as a a negative value.
