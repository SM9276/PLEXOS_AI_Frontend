Header: Physical Contract Capacity Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/kW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Hourly fixed charge for contract capacity

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Capacity Charge defines the physical contract purchase decision in LT Plan to be by interval, meaning that the contract quantity decision can change every interval of the planning horizon. The value then sets the capacity charge for purchases of generation or load capacity.
