Table Details:

Description:	Coefficient of number of units retired

Paragraphs:
Flow Node Objectives Units Retired Coefficient is the left-hand side coefficient of the Units Retired in the
          constraint.

With the coefficient represented by a (i,j) the following
          term is added to the formulation:

... + a (i,j) × Flow
            Node Units Retired ... {≤, ≥, =} b j
