Header: Physical Contract Capacity Charge Hour

Table Details:
Units:	$/kWh
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Hourly fixed charge for contract capacity

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Capacity Charge Hour is the hourly fixed charge for contract capacity. Physical Contract Capacity Charge Hour defines the physical contract purchase decision in LT Plan to be by hour, meaning that the contract quantity decision can change every hour of the planning horizon. The value then sets the capacity charge for purchases of generation or load capacity.
