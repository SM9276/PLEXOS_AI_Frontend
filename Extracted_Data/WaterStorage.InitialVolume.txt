Header: Water Storage Initial Volume

Table Details:
Units:	m³ (Metric), gal. (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	≥0
Key Property:	No
Description:	Initial volume of Water in the storage

Paragraphs:
Water Storage Initial Volume is the initial volume of Water in the storage at the start of the period.

As an input, this value applies at the start of the simulation Horizon .
