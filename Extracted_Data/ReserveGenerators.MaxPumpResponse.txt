Description:	Maximum dispatchable pump load reserve response

Paragraphs:
Reserve Generators Max Pump Response sets a limit on the response that can be made by a unit at a pumped storage generator in providing this reserve service. Pumped storage generators providing 'raise' response by decreasing Pump Load and 'lower' response by increasing Pump Load . The feasible range of pump load response is between Min Pump Load and Pump Load .

This property does not apply to replacement reserve services.
