Header: Message 3223

Table Details:
Severity:	Moderate (value = 3)
Default Action:	Warn (value = 1)
Description:	The total Gas Demand.Gas Node Demand Participation Factor does not sum to 1.0. Gas Demand: <value>, sum of Demand Participation Factor: <value>.

Paragraphs:
Message 3223 occurs in the following circumstances: When a Gas Demand - Gas Node or a Water Demand - Water Node membership has Participation Factor defined  and the total across the Nodes does not sum to 1.0.
