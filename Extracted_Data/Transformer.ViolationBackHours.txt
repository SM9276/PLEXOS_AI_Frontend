Key Property:	No
Description:	Number of hours the flow limit is violated in the counter-reference direction.

Paragraphs:
Transformer Violation Back Hours is a summary property that is the number of hours in the summary period (day/week/month/year) that the Transformer Flow is greater than the Rating in the counter-reference direction. Violations of flow limits are allowed only if the LimitPenalty is defined.
