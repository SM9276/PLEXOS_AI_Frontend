Header: Facility Efficiency Point

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	100
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Operating level associated with [Efficiency Incr]

Paragraphs:
Facility Efficiency Point is the
          operating level expressed as a proportion of Max
            Operating Level , used in combination with Efficiency
            Incr to define a custom efficiency function for the Primary
            Process conducted by the Facility overriding the Process Efficiency .
