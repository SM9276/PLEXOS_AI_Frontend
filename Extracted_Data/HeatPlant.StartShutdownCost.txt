Description:	Cost of unit starts and shutdowns

Paragraphs:
Heat plant Start & Shutdown Cost is the cost of unit starts.

When using multiple cooling states (hot, warm, cold for example), the reported start cost is based on the active cooling state.

The Start Cost referred to here includes:
Lists:
	- The Start Cost
	- The cost of Start Fuels as defined by the Offtake at Start .
