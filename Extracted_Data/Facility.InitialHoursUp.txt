Facility Initial Hours Up is used
          to set the initial ( t =0) operating state of a single-unit
          facility when used in combination with Min
            Up Time , Min Down Time , Max Up Time , Max
            Down Time or multi-band Start
            Cost . If you define this property you must also define Initial Production .
          Only one of Initial Hours Up and Initial
            Hours Down can be non-zero.
