Paragraphs:
This article aims to provide a list of permissions that are available in the PLEXOS Connect 3 system. Since these permissions may change, you can view the list of permissions available in your version of the PLEXOS Connect Server by opening the account control panel and adding or editing a user role.

Note: The default roles are intended to be additive, for example: the admin group is the combination of the roles: Review+Execute+Update+Admin.
