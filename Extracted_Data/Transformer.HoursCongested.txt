Description:	Number of hours the Transformer is flowing at its [Rating] in pre-contingency state

Paragraphs:
Transformer Hours Congested is the number of hours the Transformer Flow is at or above its Rating in either direction. Only includes hours congested for base case, i.e in pre-contingency state.

The summary version of this property reports the number of hours at Rating and Hours Congested Back the number of hours at - Rating .
