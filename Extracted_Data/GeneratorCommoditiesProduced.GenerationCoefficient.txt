Description:	Amount of the Commodity produced per unit of generation

Paragraphs:
Generator Commodities
            Produced Generation Coefficient is the amount of the Commodity produced per unit of Generation .

If Mutually Exclusive 
          Production is set as Yes, then a portion of generation of the generator is used for the commodity 
          production.
