Table Details:
Required Members:	0
Maximum Members:	unbounded
Description:	set of node heat is exported to

Paragraphs:
Heat Node Heat Export Nodes is the set of heat nodes that the Heat Node is exporting heat to.
A heat node can connect to many heat export nodes and participation factor can be defined to split the distribution of the heat or use to describe efficiency or loss.
