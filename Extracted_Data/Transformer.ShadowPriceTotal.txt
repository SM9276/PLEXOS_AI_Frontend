Description:	Shadow/expansion price

Paragraphs:
Transformer Shadow Price Total is the on the Transformer thermal limit as defined by Rating . This shadow price is derived directly from the dual solution to the mathematical programming problem used to solve the simulation. It can be interpreted as the economic benefit of relaxing the Transformer limit constraint by one megawatt. Includes shadow price for base case plus sum of post-contingency shadow prices, for all contingencies that the element is binding under.
