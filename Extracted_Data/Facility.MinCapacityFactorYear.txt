Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Minimum capacity factor in year

Paragraphs:
Facility Min Capacity Factor Year
          sets a lower bound on the facility's Capacity
            Factor in each year of the simulation. Years are defined by the
          starting month of the Horizon . See Min Capacity Factor for
          more details.
