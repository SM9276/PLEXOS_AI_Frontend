Description:	Intrinsic Commodity

Paragraphs:
The Commodity Intrinsic input defaults to None, but can also be set to Electricity, Heat, Gas, or Water. The benefit of choosing a specific type is that Universal objects can connect directly to a Node , Heat Node , Gas Node , or Water Node seamlessly.
