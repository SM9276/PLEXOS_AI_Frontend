Description:	Delivered cost of gas at the gas node

Paragraphs:
Gas Node Delivered Cost is the injection weighted cost of gas delivered at a given gas node. It is calculated by multiplying delivered price by amount of node inflow.

For details on delivered price calculations, refer to the article Citygate Pricing .
