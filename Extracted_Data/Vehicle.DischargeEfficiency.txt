Header: Vehicle Discharge Efficiency

Table Details:
Units:	%
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	100
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 100
Key Property:	No
Description:	Efficiency of discharging

Paragraphs:
As an input, Vehicle Discharge Efficiency is the ratio of power sent to the grid to the total power discharged from the vehicle including any inverter losses.

As an output, Discharge Efficiency reflects the input value less any Discharge Efficiency Degradation .
