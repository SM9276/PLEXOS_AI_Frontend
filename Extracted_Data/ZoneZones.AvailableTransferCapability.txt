Header: Zone Zones Available Transfer Capability

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Available transfer capability between the zones in the reference direction.

Paragraphs:
Zone Zones Available Transfer Capability is Available transfer capability from the 'parent' Zone to the 'child' Zone.

If Transmission Detail is set to Nodal:

Line Available Transfer Capability of the Exporting Lines and the reverse Available Transfer Capability of the Importing Lines are collected to calculate Zone Zones Available Transfer Capability.

If Transmission Detail is set to Zonal/Regional:

Zone Zones Available Transfer Capability is reported as the sum of ATCs on the inter-zonal branches.
