Description:	Cost of unit start up and shutdown

Paragraphs:
Facility Start & Shutdown Cost
          is the cost incurred for starting and shutting down units

For chronological simulation phases (see LT Plan Chronology, MT
          Schedule Chronology and ST
            Schedule ) this is:

Start & Shutdown Cost = Start
            Cost × Units Started + Shutdown Cost × Units
            Shutdown
