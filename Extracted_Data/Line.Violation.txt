Description:	Violation of flow limit

Paragraphs:
Line Violation is the amount of violation of the line thermal limits as defined by Max Flow / Max Rating (reference direction) and Min Flow / Min Rating (counter reference direction).

As a summary property, Violation is the today violation ( GWh ) in the summary period (day/week/month/year) for flows in the reference direction.

Violations can only occur if the property Limit Penalty is defined.
