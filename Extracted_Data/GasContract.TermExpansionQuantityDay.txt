Key Property:	No
Description:	LT Max daily gas offtake for the term based contract

Paragraphs:
Gas Contract Term Expansion Quantity Day is the expansion maximum daily gas offtake for the term based contract.
                For a term based contract, term window is specified using from and to dates. The contract is not available before the from date and after the to date.
                Multiple bands can be used to specify multiple terms with non-overlapping term windows. Time slices should be used to model seasonality within terms.
