Header: Company Constraints Committed Capacity Coefficient

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Coefficient of company committed generation capacity

Paragraphs:
Company Constraints Committed Capacity Coefficient is the left-hand side coefficient on the sum of committed capacity across all generators belonging to the company weighted by Share .
