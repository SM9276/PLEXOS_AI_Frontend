Header: Heat Plant Max Capacity

Table Details:
Units:	GJ/h (Metric), MMBtu/h (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum heat production

Paragraphs:
Heat Plant Max Capacity defines the maximum heat production of a heat plant per hour. Note that the heat plant will not exist if Max Capacity and Units are not defined. Heat Production =< (Max Capacity)* Units
