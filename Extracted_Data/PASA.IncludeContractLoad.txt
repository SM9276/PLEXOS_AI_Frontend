Header: PASA Include Contract Load

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	Include physical contract load capacity in PASA

Paragraphs:
PASA Include Contract Load toggles on/off inclusion of Physical Contract Load Obligation in the calculation of Region Capacity Reserves and Compute Reliability Indices .
