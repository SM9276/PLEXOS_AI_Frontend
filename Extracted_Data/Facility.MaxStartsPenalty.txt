Header: Facility Max Starts Penalty

Table Details:
Units:	$
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	-1
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Penalty applied to violations of [Max Starts] constraints

Paragraphs:
Facility Max Starts Penalty is the
          penalty applied to violations of Max
            Starts constraint and its period variants ( Hour , Day , Week , Month , and Year ). The value is the
          penalty applied to each Units
            Started above the given limit. The default value of -1 means "no
          violation allowed". Violations are reported in the properties Max
            Starts Violation and Max
            Starts Violation Cost .
