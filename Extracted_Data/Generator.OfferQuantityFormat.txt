Header: Generator Offer Quantity Format

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	In (0,1)
Key Property:	No
Description:	Format for [Offer Quantity] and [Offer Price]

Paragraphs:
Generator Offer Quantity Format is the format for Offer Quantity and Offer Price .

The total quantity is the sum of offer/bid quantity across the bands. You must define the same number of bands for quantity and price.

The total is the offer quantity/bid in the top band i.e. the offer quantities are similar to load points (see Load Point ). You must define one more quantity band than price band.

See the Offer Quantity topic for examples.
