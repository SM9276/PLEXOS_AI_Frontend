Header: Market Is Forward

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Key Property:	No
Description:	Flag if the market is a 'forward' market versus a 'real-time' market.

Paragraphs:
Indicates if the market is a forward market (true) or a real-time market (false). The forward markets are solved first, followed by the real-time markets in a two-stage optimization as described in the Market topic.
