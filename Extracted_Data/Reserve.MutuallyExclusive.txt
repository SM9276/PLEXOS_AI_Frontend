Header: Reserve Mutually Exclusive

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	In (0,1,2)
Key Property:	No
Description:	If generation capacity providing this reserve is mutually exclusive to other reserves

Paragraphs:
Reserve Mutually Exclusive applies
          to reserves based on spare production capacity and controls whether or
          not the capacity reserved for this service can also be used to provide
          reserve to other 'like' services. Note that the mutually exclusive
          limit is based on the spare capacity and not the Max
            Response values for the individual reserve objects.
