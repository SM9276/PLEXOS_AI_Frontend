Description:	Fixed dispatch cost component of generator offer.

Paragraphs:
Generator Offer No Load Cost is the fixed dispatch cost component of generator offer. If the Region MLF Adjusts No Load Cost property is set, this cost is modified by the Marginal Loss Factor .
