Header: Generator Declining Depreciation Balance

Table Details:

Description:	Balance applied to declining depreciation method

Paragraphs:
Generator Declining Depreciation Balance is used by the LT Plan capacity expansion algorithm when computing annuities for generator Build Cost when the Depreciation Method is set to "Declining Balance".
