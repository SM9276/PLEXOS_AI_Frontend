Header: Physical Contract Capacity Charge Week

Table Details:
Units:	$/kW/week
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Weekly fixed charge for contract capacity

Paragraphs:
Physical Contract Capacity Charge Week defines the physical contract purchase decision in LT Plan to be by week, meaning that the contract quantity decision can change every week of the planning horizon. The value then sets the capacity charge for purchases of generation or load capacity.
