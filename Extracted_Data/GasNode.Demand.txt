Description:	Demand at the gas node

Paragraphs:
Gas Node Demand is the total Gas Demand Demand at the Gas Node .

Gas Node Demand = Gas Demand + Fuel Offtake + Market Sales

The actual quantity of gas served is the net of this value at the Shortage and Excess .
