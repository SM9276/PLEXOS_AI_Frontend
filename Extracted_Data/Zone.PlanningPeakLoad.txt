Description:	Peak load in the current capacity optimization period (e.g. year)

Paragraphs:
Zone Planning Peak Load is an output of LT Plan . It is the peak load used in calculation of reserve margins and in enforcing reserve margin constraints such as Min Capacity Reserve Margin . LT Plan enforces these constraints for the peak period in each year of the horizon. The Planning Peak Load is defined as:

All taken at the time of maximum Load in the planning year.
