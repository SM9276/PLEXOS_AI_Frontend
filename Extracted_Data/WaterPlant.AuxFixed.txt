Header: Water Plant Aux Fixed

Table Details:
Units:	m³/day (Metric), gal./day (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	≥0
Key Property:	No
Description:	Fixed auxiliary usage per installed unit

Paragraphs:
Water Plant Aux Fixed represents fixed internal water usage of a water plant even when it is operating or not.
