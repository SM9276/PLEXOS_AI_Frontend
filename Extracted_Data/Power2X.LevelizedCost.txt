Header: Power2X Levelized Cost

Table Details:
Units:	$/GJ (Metric), $/MMBtu (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Levelized cost of the commodity produced

Paragraphs:
Power2X Levelized Cost is
          the average total cost of building and operating the Power2X facility
          per unit of the commodity produced over the assumed lifetime, and is
          calculated as:

Levelized Cost = ( Total
            Production Cost + Fixed Cost )
          / Production
