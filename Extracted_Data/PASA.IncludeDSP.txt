Header: PASA Include DSP

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	True (-1)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	Include demand-side participation in PASA

Paragraphs:
PASA Include DSP toggles on/off counting of Region demand-side participation (DSP) and Node DSP in Region Capacity Reserves .
