Header: Power Station Nodes Generation Participation Factor

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1
Validation Rule:	Between 0 And 1
Key Property:	No
Description:	Proportion of generator load injected at the node

Paragraphs:
Power Station Nodes Generation Participation Factor is the proportion of generator load injected at the node. This is used to override the participation factor that is calculated when aggregating the Generators in the Power Station .
