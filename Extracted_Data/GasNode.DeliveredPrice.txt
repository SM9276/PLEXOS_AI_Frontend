Description:	Delivered price for gas at the gas node

Paragraphs:
Gas Node Delivered Price is the output of Gas Citygate Pricing functionality in PLEXOS. The blend of gas appearing at each gas node is priced based on injection weights and corresponding prices of node injections.

For details on delivered price calculations, refer to the article Citygate Pricing .
