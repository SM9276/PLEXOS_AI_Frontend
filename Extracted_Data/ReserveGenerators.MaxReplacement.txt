Description:	Maximum replacement (non-spinning) reserve capability

Paragraphs:
Reserve Generators Max Replacement defines the maximum response that the generating unit can provide to the replacement reserve requirement. By default a generator can provide its entire off-line capacity up to the defined Rating as replacement reserves.
