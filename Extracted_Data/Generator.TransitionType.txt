Table Details:
Units:	-

Multi-band:	False

Description:	If the generator can transition between a single generator or to a group of generators.

Paragraphs:
Setting Transition Type If the generator can transition between a single generator (Individual) or to a group of generators (Group).
See also Transition and Transition Cost Note that if the setting is "Group", the cost will be the sum of all individual transition cost.
