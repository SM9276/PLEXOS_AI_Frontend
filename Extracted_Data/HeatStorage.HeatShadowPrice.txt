Description:	Shadow price of heat in storage

Paragraphs:
Heat Storage Heat Shadow Price refers to the dual variable value representing the additional (Maximum, usual) cost for an extra unit of Heat Storage capacity. This value indicates the marginal cost of the last or next unit of Heat Storage capacity required or used in the system.
