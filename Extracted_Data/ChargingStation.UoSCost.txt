Description:	Total use of system cost

Paragraphs:
Charging Stations UoS Cost
          is the total use of system cost incurred by the charging station from
          charging and discharging from/to the grid. It is defined as:

UoS Cost = UoS
            Charge x ( Charging + Discharging )
