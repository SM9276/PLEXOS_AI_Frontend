Description:	Number of hours the Transformer is flowing at its [Rating]

Paragraphs:
Transformer Hours Congested Total is the number of hours the Transformer Flow is at or above its Rating in either direction. Includes hours congested for base case and post-contingent hours.

The summary version of this property reports the number of hours at Rating and Hours Congested Back the number of hours at - Rating .
