Description:	Energy from the Commodity

Paragraphs:
Vehicle Commodities Energy Consumption is the amount of energy consumed by the vehicle
          using the given Commodity. It is defined as:

Energy Consumption = Ratio x Distance x ( Efficiency / 1000)
