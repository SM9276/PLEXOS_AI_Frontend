Description:	Maximum quantity of gas that can be extracted from the pipeline each day

Paragraphs:
Gas Pipeline Max Flow DAY is the daily version of Gas Pipeline Max Flow .

Gas Pipeline Max Flow Day is the maximum quantity of gas that can be extracted from the pipeline each day. This includes gas flowing through the pipeline as well as gas taken from storage in the pipeline i.e. it puts an upper limit on the Flow .
