Header: Gas Demand Gas Nodes Demand Participation Factor

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Proportion of gas demand that occurs at the gas node

Paragraphs:
Gas Demand Gas Nodes Demand Participation Factor is the proportion of gas Demand that occurs at the Gas Node .

If the Gas Demand is connected to one Gas Node the default value for the participation factor is one, otherwise the default value is zero i.e. if you connect a Gas Demand to multiple Gas Nodes you should set these participation factors to non-zero values.
