Header: Node Discrete Maintenance

Table Details:
Units:	MW

Key Property:	No
Description:	Discrete maintenance (defined by Units Out)

Paragraphs:
Node Discrete Maintenance is reported by PASA . It is the sum of all maintenance defined by the Generator Units Out property for all generators at that Node .
