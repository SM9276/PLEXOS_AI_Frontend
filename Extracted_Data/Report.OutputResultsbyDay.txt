Header: Report Output Results by Day

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	False (0)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	If summary results are written by day

Paragraphs:
Report Output Results by Day switches on/off writing of summary results for each 'trading day' of the simulation horizon where the trading day is defined by the setting Day Beginning .
