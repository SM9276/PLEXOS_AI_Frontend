Description:	Shadow price on the Company [Strategic] constraint.

Paragraphs:
This is the shadow price associated with the constraint created to enforce the Company Strategic property. It represents the marginal cost of that constraint on the companys generation, or perhaps more intuitively the discount being applied to the companys generation in order to keep its non-strategic capacity generating.
