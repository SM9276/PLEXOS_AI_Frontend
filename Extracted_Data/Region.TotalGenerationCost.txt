Description:	Total generation cost including fuel, VO&M, start and shutdown costs and emissions costs.

Paragraphs:
Region Total Generation Cost is the total generation cost including fuel, variable operations and maintenance costs, start and shutdown costs and emissions costs and is defined as:

Total Generation Cost = Generation Cost + Start & Shutdown Cost + Emissions Cost .

Note that Generation Cost includes generation costs from Generators , Generation Contracts and Batteries .
