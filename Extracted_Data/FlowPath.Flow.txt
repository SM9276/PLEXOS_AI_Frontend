Description:	Net flow of the Commodity

Paragraphs:
Flow Path Flow is the net flow of
          the Commodity is the notional
          forward direction i.e. from the Flow
            Node From to the Flow Node
            To . It is defined as:

Flow = Flow Forward - Flow Back

Note that this is the 'sent out' flow prior to any Losses .
          The flow received is given by:

Flow (received) = Flow - Losses
