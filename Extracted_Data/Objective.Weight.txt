Description:	Weight of the objective when doing blended multi-objective optimization

Paragraphs:
Objective Weight is Weight of the objective when doing blended multi-objective optimization. The objectives that have the same priority in hierarchical optimization are merged to form a blended objective (eg: w1*o1+w2*o2)
