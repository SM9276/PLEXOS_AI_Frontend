Header: Competition Non Price Setting Enabled

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	True (-1)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	If non-price setting plant should be fixed prior to the final price calculation

Paragraphs:
Competition Non Price Setting determines if non-price setting plants should be excluded from the 
          final price pricing calculation. If the setting is turned on, non-price setting plants are fixed to their optimal solution 
          and a final pricing optimization is solved.
