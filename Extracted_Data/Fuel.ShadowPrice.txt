Header: Fuel Shadow Price

Table Details:
Units:	$/~
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Shadow price of fuel (if defined as input, sets the internal price for fuel)

Paragraphs:
Fuel Shadow Price is the dual variable associated with the fuel and represents the marginal value of more fuel supply to the system overall. This reflects the price of the fuel and any defined constraints or fuel contracts .
