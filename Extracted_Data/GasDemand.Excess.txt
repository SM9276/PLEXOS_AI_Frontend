Header: Gas Demand Excess

Table Details:
Units:	GJ (Metric), MMBtu (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Excess of gas supply

Paragraphs:
Gas Demand Excess is the amount by which supply of gas exceeds the Demand . This excess has a notional price set by the Excess Price . The associated cost is reported in the Excess Cost property.
