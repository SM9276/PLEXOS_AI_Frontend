Header: Generator Mark-to-Markets

Table Details:
Required Members:	0
Maximum Members:	unbounded
Description:	market used to mark-to-market energy and reserves

Paragraphs:
Generator Mark-to-Markets collection defines the set of Market that mark-to-market the generators generation or ancillary services. The Price Received is then set by the Market with a Nodes membership, while ancillary service prices received are set by Markets with Reserves memberships.
