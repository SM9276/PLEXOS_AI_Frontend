Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Quantity of contract settled

Paragraphs:
Financial Contract Settlement Quantity is the volume of the contract used to compute the settlement . This varies depending on the setting of Contracts Settlement Method .

Additional quantities can be added to the contract (over and above the Quantity input) by associating load and/or generation with the contract.
