Header: Generator Constraints Net Revenue Coefficient

Table Details:
Units:	$
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Coefficient of net revenue

Paragraphs:
Generator Constraints Net Revenue Coefficient is Coefficient of net revenue.

The Heat Market Revenue, Heat Revenue, Reserve Revenue, and emission cost are not included in this property. Only constant heat rate is supported in generation cost component.
