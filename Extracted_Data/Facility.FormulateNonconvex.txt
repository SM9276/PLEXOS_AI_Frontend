Paragraphs:
Facility Formulate Non-convex
          controls when integer variables and constraints are formulated so that
          the marginal efficiency tranches ( Efficiency
            Point and Efficiency
            Incr ) are despatched in order.

Settings other than 'Auto' supersede the Heat
            Rate Error Method setting in the Production object.
