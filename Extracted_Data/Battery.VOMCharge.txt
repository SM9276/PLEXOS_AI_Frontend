Header: Battery VO&M Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/MWh
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Variable operation and maintenance charge

Paragraphs:
Battery VO&M Charge is the variable operation and maintenance charge. It applies to the gross amount of Charging and Discharging . However, an additional Charging VO&M Charge or Discharging VO&M Charge which may be added to VO&M Charge, applies to the gross amount of charging or discharging.
