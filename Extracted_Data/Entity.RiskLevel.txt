Header: Entity Risk Level

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0.1
Validation Rule:	Between 0 and 1
Key Property:	No
Description:	Risk level for risk-constrained optimization

Paragraphs:
Entity Risk Level works with Acceptable
            Risk to set the lowest acceptable Entity Net Profit . Commonly used values
          are 1% (0.01), 5% (0.05) and 10% (0.1). Higher values are more risk
          averse. See Formulate Risk for details.
