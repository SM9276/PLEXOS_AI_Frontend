Header: Generator Heat Rate

Table Details:
Units:	GJ/MWh (Metric), Btu/kWh (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input/Output
Multi-band:	True
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Average heat rate (total fuel divided by total generation)

Paragraphs:
Generator Heat Rate is the average heat rate used in defining the heat rate function .

Output Heat Rate is the average heat rate value at Max Capacity including no-load heat rate.
