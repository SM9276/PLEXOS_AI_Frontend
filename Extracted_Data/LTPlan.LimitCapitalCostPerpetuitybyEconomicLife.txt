Header: LT Plan Limit Capital Cost Perpetuity by Economic Life

Table Details:
Units:	-
Default Value:	True (-1)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	With perpetuity end effect, if annuity calculation should extend beyond the end of the horizon up to the Economic Life

Paragraphs:
LT Plan Limit Capital Cost Perpetuity by Economic Life controls how annuities are calculated when the Perpetuity End Effects Method is selected. This setting can take the following values:

Annuity calculation for capital cost extends beyond the end of the horizon, but the term of annuity is limited by the Economic Life.

The perpetuity logic applies to annuities.
