Description:	Fixed supply (exact amount of supply from the Market)

Paragraphs:
Market Supply defines a fixed
          'supply' or exact amount of purchases required from the Market.
          Shortages are priced at the Shortage
            Price while surpluses are priced at the Surplus
            Price .
