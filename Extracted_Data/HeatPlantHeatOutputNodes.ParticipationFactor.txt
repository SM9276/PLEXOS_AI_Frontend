Header: Heat Plant Heat Output Nodes Participation Factor

Table Details:
Units:	-
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1
Validation Rule:	Between -1 And 1
Key Property:	No
Description:	Proportion sent to the node

Paragraphs:
Heat Output Nodes Participation Factor is the proportion of heat output injected at the heat node. Using this property you can split the heat output of a heat plant across multiple Heat Output Nodes.
It can also be used to described the efficiency of waste heat recovery in a heat plant.
