Header: Vehicle Energy

Table Details:
Units:	kWh
Mode:	Output Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	
Validation Rule:	
Key Property:	No
Description:	Energy stored

Paragraphs:
Vehicle Energy is the energy stored
          in the battery at the end of the reporting period. It is calculated as
          follows:

Energy t = Energy t -1 + Charging t - Discharging t - Demand t x Efficiency / 1000
