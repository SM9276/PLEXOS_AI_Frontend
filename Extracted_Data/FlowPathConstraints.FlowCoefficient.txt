Paragraphs:
Flow Path Constraints Flow Coefficient is the left-hand side coefficient of the Flow in the constraint.

With the coefficient represented by a (i,j) the following
          term is added to the formulation:

... + a (i,j) × Flow
            Path Flow ... {≤,
            ≥, =} b j
