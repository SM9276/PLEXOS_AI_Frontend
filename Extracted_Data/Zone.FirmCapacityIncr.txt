Header: Zone Firm Capacity Incr

Table Details:
Units:	MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Firm Capacity not explicitly modeled that should be included in reserve margin calculations

Paragraphs:
Zone Firm Capacity Incr represents capacity not explicitly modeled that should be included in Capacity Reserve Margin calculations by adding to Firm Generation Capacity . In LT Plan this will have the effect of reducing capacity required to meet Min Capacity Reserve Margin .
