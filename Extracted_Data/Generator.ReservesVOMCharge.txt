Header: Generator Reserves VO&M Charge

Table Details:
Units:	$/MW
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	Any Value
Key Property:	No
Description:	Variable O&M cost associated with providing spinning reserve

Paragraphs:
Generator Reserves VO&M Charge is a variable operations and maintenance cost associated with provision of spinning and replacement reserves. The cost incurred is reported as Reserves VO&M Cost .

See also Reserve Type , VO&M Charge .
