Paragraphs:
Facility Fixed Production
          Violation is the violation of the Fixed Production constraint and is defined as:

Fixed Production Violation = Production - Fixed Production

or (for expansion facilities in LT Plan ):

Fixed Production Violation = Production - Fixed Production x Units
