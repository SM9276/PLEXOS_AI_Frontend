Header: Gas Pipeline Max Flow Year

Table Details:
Units:	1000·~ (Metric), ~ (U.S. Imperial)
Mode:	Input Only
Multi-band:	False
Default Value:	1E+30
Validation Rule:	≥0
Key Property:	No
Description:	Maximum quantity of gas that can be extracted from the pipeline each year

Paragraphs:
Gas Pipeline Max Flow YEAR is the yearly version of Gas Pipeline Max Flow .

Gas Pipeline Max Flow YEAR is the maximum quantity of gas that can be extracted from the pipeline annually. This includes gas flowing through the pipeline as well as gas taken from storage in the pipeline i.e. it puts an upper limit on the Flow .
