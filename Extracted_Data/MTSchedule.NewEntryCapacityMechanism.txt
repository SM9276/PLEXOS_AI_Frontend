Header: MT Schedule New Entry Capacity Mechanism

Table Details:
Units:	-
Default Value:	0
Validation Rule:	In (0,1,2)
Description:	Capacity payment mechanism.

Paragraphs:
MT Schedule New Entry Capacity Mechanism applies when the New Entry Driver option is enabled. It controls whether or not new entry candidates will receive an out-of-market capacity payment or incentive to provide capacity, over and above any premium available from generation in the pool.
