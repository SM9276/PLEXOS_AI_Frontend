Header: Transmission Report All Interregional Flows

Table Details:
Units:	Yes/No
Default Value:	True (-1)
Validation Rule:	False (0) or True (-1)
Description:	If line flows between regions should be reported regardless of kV.

Paragraphs:
Transmission Report All Interregional Flows turns on reporting of transmission lines that connect regions regardless of their voltage and setting of Transmission Report Voltage Threshold .
