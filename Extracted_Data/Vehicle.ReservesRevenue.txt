Description:	Revenue from provision of reserves

Paragraphs:
Vehicle Reserves Revenue is the
          revenue earned from providing Reserves (ancillary services) through the Charging
            Stations . It is defined as:

Reserves Revenue = Raise
            Reserve x Price + Lower
            Reserve x Price + Regulation
            Raise Reserve x Price + Regulation
            Lower Reserve x Price + Replacement
            Reserve x Price
