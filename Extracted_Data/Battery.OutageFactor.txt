Header: Battery Outage Factor

Table Details:
Units:	%

Description:	Battery outage rating based on max power

Paragraphs:
Battery Outage Factor sets the
          outage rating of each unit
          during the associated Forced or Maintenance outage as a
          proportion of the Max Power .
