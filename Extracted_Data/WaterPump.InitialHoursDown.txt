Water Pump Initial Hours Down is
          used to set the initial ( t =0) operating state of a
          pump when used in combination with Min
            Up Time , Min Down Time , Max Up Time , Max
            Down Time . Only one of Initial Hours Down and Initial
            Hours Up can be non-zero.
